What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence by machines, especially computer systems. These processes include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and even understanding language. AI is revolutionizing industries from healthcare to entertainment and beyond.
